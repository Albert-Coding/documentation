### `--retention`

Set the [Retention Period](/clusters#retention-period) for the [Namespace](/concepts/what-is-a-namespace).

The Retention Period applies to Closed [Workflow Executions](/concepts/what-is-a-workflow-execution).

Alias `--rd`
